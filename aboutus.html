About Us

Welcome to the District Institute of Education & Training (DIET), Chennai, a premier institution dedicated to enhancing teacher education and educational excellence. Established in 1991, DIET Chennai is located on the Lady Willingdon Campus at Kamarajar Salai, Triplicane, Chennai, directly across from Marina Beach. This location is not just scenic but historic, reflecting our commitment to an enduring legacy of quality in education.

DIET Chennai was founded as part of a national initiative under the National Policy on Education, 1986, which envisioned strong district-level institutions to uplift teacher training and foster educational development. We offer the Diploma in Teacher Education, a pre-service program recognized by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE). This course aims to equip future educators with foundational skills and knowledge, setting them on a path toward successful careers in teaching.

Our mission extends beyond initial teacher training. DIET Chennai collaborates closely with schools and the State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) to develop curriculum frameworks that meet the changing needs of students and align with national educational goals. We are committed to driving educational innovation, supporting schools with targeted training programs, and providing teachers with regular refresher courses, workshops, and seminars to foster continuous professional growth.

Today, DIET Chennai is a recognized center for academic excellence and professional development in Tamil Nadu. While our mission continues to evolve, our dedication to advancing education remains steadfast.
